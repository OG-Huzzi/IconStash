IconStash.io is a unified icon finder and aggregation tool designed for developers, UI/UX designers, and digital creators. Instead of navigating multiple websites to piece together your visual language, you can search across 28+ of the most popular open-source icon libraries with ease. Every icon indexed on IconStash is open-source (MIT, Apache 2.0, etc.), meaning they are entirely free to use in both your personal and commercial projects.

We engineered the platform to be a completely serverless, frontend-only experience. This means your searches are blazing fast and your customizations happen securely within your own browser. From tweaking stroke widths and hex codes to downloading high-resolution PNG ZIP archives or copying inline SVGs, IconStash provides a premium, zero-latency toolset — no account, no subscription, or server pings required. Just free, open-source icons with a lightning-fast search!
